# MVP Scope

## Product Goal

Launch a controlled pilot platform where a patient can compare verified clinics, upload medical documents with explicit consent, receive an AI-assisted summary with citations, request a quote, communicate inside the platform and pay a deposit.

## MVP Countries and Specialties

Start narrow to reduce medical, legal and operational risk:

- Countries: United Kingdom, France, Spain, plus one non-EU destination only after data-transfer and liability review.
- Specialties: dental care, ophthalmology or non-urgent cosmetic surgery first.
- Exclude minors for MVP.
- Avoid fertility, emergency care, autonomous diagnosis and autonomous prescription.

## Must-Have Capabilities

- Patient account, profile, preferences and consent onboarding.
- Role-based access for patient, clinic, doctor, admin, support and DPO.
- Procedure catalogue with disclaimers and required documents.
- Verified clinic profiles and clinic procedure pricing.
- Search, filtering, comparison and estimated total cost.
- Secure document vault for PDF, JPEG, PNG and later DOCX.
- OCR and AI-generated summary with source citations.
- Patient review/correction before sharing.
- Quote request workflow with clinic accept/reject/more-documents states.
- Masked messaging and platform-controlled video links.
- Deposit or booking-fee payment through a PCI-compliant provider.
- Medication reminders and basic post-operative journal.
- Admin back-office for verification, audit logs, support and DPO export.

## Explicit Non-Goals

- No autonomous diagnosis or treatment recommendation.
- No direct medication sale.
- No insurance sale without a regulated partner.
- No travel package sale until legal review is complete.
- No use of patient documents for AI training by default.
- No clinic access to documents without explicit, logged consent.

## MVP Acceptance Bar

- Patient can compare at least 10 verified clinic offers.
- Patient can share documents only through explicit consent.
- Clinic can review, request more documents, accept, reject and quote.
- Contact details are masked before booking unless medically necessary.
- AI summary cites document, page and date where possible.
- Deposit payment is processed without storing card data.
- Audit logs are available for document access, consent and admin actions.
- Data export and deletion request paths exist.
