# MVP Backlog

## Epic 1 - Identity, Roles and Consent

- Email login for patients.
- MFA for clinic/admin/support/DPO roles.
- Role-based permissions.
- Consent ledger for document upload, AI analysis, clinic sharing, medication follow-up and marketing opt-in.
- Audit log for authentication, consent and sensitive access.

## Epic 2 - Patient Profile

- Country, language, currency and travel preferences.
- Medical history, allergies, current medication and previous procedures.
- Emergency contact as a later MVP enhancement.
- Data export and deletion/deletion-request workflow.

## Epic 3 - Catalogue and Clinic Network

- Specialty and procedure catalogue.
- Procedure pages with general information and medical-advice disclaimers.
- Clinic onboarding and verification.
- Doctor verification.
- Clinic procedure pricing with included/excluded items.

## Epic 4 - Search, Comparison and Cost Estimation

- Filter clinics by country, price, availability, language, accreditation and rating.
- Estimated total cost: procedure, flight estimate, hotel estimate and transfers.
- Compare up to four clinics.
- Clearly label estimates and "from" prices.

## Epic 5 - Secure Document Vault and AI Summary

- Upload PDF, JPEG and PNG.
- Store encrypted documents outside the database.
- OCR extraction with confidence.
- AI summary with allergies, history, medication, diagnoses, results, timeline and questions.
- Source citations and uncertainty list.
- Patient review, correction and hide-before-share controls.

## Epic 6 - Quote, Messaging and Video

- Quote requests to selected clinics.
- Clinic states: request more documents, reject, propose consultation, issue quote.
- Mask direct contact details before booking.
- Platform-controlled video consultation link.
- Support escalation for bypass or abuse.

## Epic 7 - Payment and Booking

- Quote acceptance.
- Deposit or booking-fee payment with PSP.
- Receipt generation.
- Booking confirmation and procedure calendar.
- Refund/cancellation policy acknowledgement.

## Epic 8 - Medication and Post-Op

- Medication plan: name, dose, frequency, duration and instructions.
- Reminder scheduling.
- Intake states: taken, missed, postponed, side effect.
- Basic post-operative journal.
- Clinic view only with active consent.

## Epic 9 - Admin, DPO and Compliance

- Clinic and doctor verification dashboard.
- Audit log search.
- Document access review.
- Support case view with justified access.
- DPO export and retention tools.
- Incident logging for AI, privacy and security events.
